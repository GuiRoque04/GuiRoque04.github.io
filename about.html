Gui Roque

I specialize in creating up-close, intimate portraits. Whether it's a casual session or a professional shoot, my goal is to make you comfortable in front of the camera and deliver portraits that resonate with your personality.

I started taking photos back in late 2022 and have since been improving my skills and style. I take inspiration from today's entertainment, whether it be celebrities, movies, TV shows, or video games. I always draw inspiration from something.

I also take inspiration from Yulia Gorbchenko and Jack Bridgeland. Their styles and vision are something I really gravitate toward and want to add my own spin to. Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch for your next memorable photos of yourself.
