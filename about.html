About me-



I'm Gui, a portrait photographer based in Iceland.

I specialize in creating up and close intimate portraits that reflect the beauty that is in everyone of us. Whether it's a casual session or a professional shoot, my goal is to make you comfortable in front of the camera and deliver portraits that resonate with your personality.

I started taking photos back in late 2022, and have since been improving my skill and style.

"It's not the camera but who's behind it that makes for a good photograph." - Ken Rockwell. Though maybe a little bit chessy it is true. I use a Canon EOS 400D, with its stock lens and a zoom lens.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch for your next memorable images of you.
