Are you wondering how this works ?

First you contact me. Through Instagram or email, where our photoshoot process begins with a chat to explore ideas if you don't have any in mind. We'll work together to decide on outfits and on a cool place for the shoot.

When the shoot day arrives, we'll take lots of pictures. Afterward, you'll get X number of photos to keep and use however you like. I'll also pick some photos that I think are great for the project I create out of our shoot, and if you're happy with them, I'll use those.

It's all about making sure you feel comfortable and that the pictures we take really capture your style and story. I'm here to make sure you're happy with the whole process from start to finish.

How much does it cost ?

I actually do most of my photoshoots for free. I do this because it helps me get better at what I love—photography. It's like adding pieces to my photo collection while I learn new things.

But when it's a private event, things change a bit. That's when I work entirely for the client, for you. It's not just about taking pictures; it's about making sure I capture exactly what you want and make your event look amazing.

Doing these paid gigs shows how serious I am about my work. It's all about giving you the best experience and making sure your special moments are captured beautifully.

That doen't mean my normal usually free photoshoots come out any worse. It's just a more casual side of my work, where there is less pressure and the main objective is to keep things fun and exiting.
