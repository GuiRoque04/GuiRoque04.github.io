FAQ

Do I need to have modeled before?

No, I'll guide you step by step through the processes, such as posing, and together we'll create amazing photos.

How do we pick an outfit?

Outfit selection is a joint effort, but usually, I prefer you to bring something from your wardrobe to showcase your unique style.

How many photos will I receive?

You will recive at least 40 untouched and 10 more edited photos, from our shoot.

Who selects the photos?

You will receive a minimum of 40 untouched and an additional 10 edited photos from our shoot

When will I get my photos?

Magic takes time, and each project varies. It could take around a week or two.

Who do you photograph?

I photograph everyone: men, women, couples, friends, families, and also offer maternity sessions!

What about companies, artists and brands?

I've worked on song cover arts and product model photography. Commercially, I'm open to various photography projects.
