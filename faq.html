FAQ

Do I need to have modeled before?

No. I'll guide you step by step, through the processes such a posing, and we'll create amazing photos.

How do we pick an outfit?

Outfit selection is a joint effort. But ussually, I like to just have you come with something from your wardrobe, to showcase your unique style.

How many photos will I receive?

You will recive at least 40 untouched and 10 more edited photos, from our shoot.

Who selects the photos?

Together we pick 10 for me to edit and post.

When will i get my photos?

Magic takes time, and each project is different. It could take a week or two.

Who do you photograph?

I photograph everyone— men, women, couples, friends, families and maternity sessions too!

What about artists and brands?

Yes, I photograph for artists and brands, like song cover arts, or product model photography.
